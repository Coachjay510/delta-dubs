THE FILM ROOM
COACHES NEED

Designed around the dual-camera workflow AAU coaches actually use. One camera per basket. Sync them up. Edit both in one window.

  • Dual-camera sync — load Cam 1 and Cam 2, mark a sync point, and both play together in perfect lockstep
  • 3 view modes — PiP overlay, side-by-side comparison, and cut mode for timeline editing
  • Blade mode — click anywhere on the timeline to cut without lifting your hands off the keyboard
  • Live stat logging — log shots, rebounds, assists while watching film. Shot chart auto-updates with every pin
  • Full-court shot chart with makes/misses, player names, and auto court-flip at halftime
  • SportsCenter-style stat card overlays — player photo, name, number, and auto-pulled stats
  • Export clips via FFmpeg — cut exact moments and save to your highlight reel folder
